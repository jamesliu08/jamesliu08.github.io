Welcome to Prism Consulting

A premier pre-professional student organization at the University of Chicago

Training Program

Prism Consulting provides motivated college students considering careers in business with the resources and mentorship they need. Our quarterly Consulting Training Program introduces students to the wide world of consulting, breaks down the consulting recruiting process, and advises eager young professionals on their paths to success in the business world.

Pro Bono Consulting

Our Pro Bono Division engages in quarterly projects with various startups, nonprofits, and corporate clients. We seek innovative solutions to pressing business problems. Teams partner with organizations across diverse industries to drive forward specific initiatives, whether through market and competitor analysis, marketing and social media development, or other data analysis and research.

Case Competitions

Prism partners with prestigious management consulting firms to organize two case interview competitions a year. These competitions provide an opportunity for aspiring consultants to garner consulting interview experience, hone their casing skills, and interact with experienced consulting professionals. In the past, finalists received prizes such as networking dinners and first round interviews.
